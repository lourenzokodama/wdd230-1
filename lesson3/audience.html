Target Audience

Description of Target Audience

The audience that I will be targeting will be governement officials working in the local government and normal every day indivuals who would be looking for information regarding their chamber of commerce.

Personas

Sam

Sam who is a county administrator looking to working with the Chamber of Commerce on projects around the county and city that they live in. Sam is a down-to-earth individual who works hard and wants to get to the point. He has a master's degree in economics and would like to get more information on meetings the Chamber may be having.

Sally

Sally is a mom who has a small business. She is wanting to attend the upcoming meeting regarding small businesses in the area. She is wondering if there is a place to sign up so that she makes sure she can get into the main conference room before it fills up.

Scenarios

There is a Chamber of Commerce meeting coming up where the entire county is invited. As it is a public forum, the officials want to have all information readily available and on the front page if possible.

Targeted to the entire Bend community
